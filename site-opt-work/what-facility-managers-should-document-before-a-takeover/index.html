What Facility Managers Should Document Before a Takeover

Good documentation shortens takeover time and improves remediation accuracy.

Direct Answer

Capture system inventory, network details, credential ownership, and open defects before new teams mobilize. This reduces troubleshooting time.

Practical Steps

  • Export available system configs and user lists
  • Record network, VLAN, and power dependencies
  • Map failed devices and incomplete areas
  • Collect permits, inspection notes, and change logs
  • Align decision owners for approval checkpoints
