Structured Cabling Checklist for Tenant Improvement Projects

A standardized checklist helps avoid failed cutovers and avoidable change orders.

Direct Answer

Define pathway, rack, termination, labeling, and testing standards before install begins. Most TI delays come from unclear standards and late coordination.

Practical Steps

  • Confirm pathway and conduit routes before rough-in
  • Set rack elevation and patching conventions
  • Define labeling format and ownership
  • Require test results and punch-list closeout process
  • Coordinate handoff package before occupancy
