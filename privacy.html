Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026

Overview

Logic Weise LLC uses this landing page to provide information about our commercial technology integration services and to receive project review requests. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

This page is intended for commercial inquiries. If you submit information through our project review form, we use that information to review your request, respond to you, and manage the related business inquiry.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information through this landing page:

  • Contact information such as your name, phone number, email address, company name, and city or project location.
  • Commercial project information that you choose to provide, such as service needs, scope details, timing, and related notes.
  • Communications information when you call, email, or otherwise contact us about a project.
  • Basic technical information automatically generated when you visit the site, such as IP address, browser type, and device or network information made available through normal website requests.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to project review requests and follow up about your project.
  • Create and manage inquiry records in our customer relationship management tools.
  • Send internal email notifications so our team can review and respond to submissions.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the landing page and related business processes.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our business, visitors, and systems.
We do not currently use this landing page form to add visitors to a marketing email list. If we introduce marketing email signups in the future, we will update this policy and provide the disclosures and opt-out controls required by applicable law.

How Inquiry Data Is Processed

Project review requests submitted through this site are processed through a Pipedrive web form. Based on our current setup, form submissions create or update records in our CRM workflow and also trigger email notifications so we can respond to your request.

We may also use service providers that help us host, secure, maintain, or support this website and our inquiry handling processes. Those providers may process information on our behalf as needed to perform those services.

Sharing of Information

We do not currently sell personal information collected through this landing page.

We may disclose information in the following situations:

  • To service providers and vendors that help us operate the site, receive form submissions, or manage communications.
  • To comply with legal process, law enforcement requests, or other legal obligations.
  • To protect the rights, safety, security, or property of Logic Weise LLC, our visitors, or others.
  • As part of a business transition if ownership or operations are reorganized, sold, or transferred.

Retention

We keep inquiry information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request, maintain business records, manage customer and project communications, and comply with legal, tax, insurance, or operational requirements.

We aim to collect only the information reasonably needed for a commercial inquiry and to limit access to that information to the people and providers who need it for legitimate business purposes.

Your Choices and Privacy Requests

Depending on where you live and the laws that apply, you may have rights related to access, correction, deletion, or other control over personal information. To make a privacy request, contact us using the contact details below and describe your request with enough detail for us to review it.

If we deny a request that is subject to an appeal right under applicable law, you may reply to our response email within 30 days and state that you would like to appeal the decision.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information handled through this landing page and related business systems. No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, contact Logic Weise LLC at DTXadmin@LogicWeise.com or (945)-214-6222.

If our data practices materially change, we may update this policy and revise the effective date above.
