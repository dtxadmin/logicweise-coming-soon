Accessibility

Updated: March 30, 2026

Our Commitment

Logic Weise LLC is committed to making this website more accessible and usable for visitors with a wide range of abilities and assistive technologies. We want people to be able to learn about our services, review our contact information, and request a consultation without unnecessary barriers.

Accessibility Efforts

We are working to support accessibility through steps such as:

  • Using meaningful headings, readable color contrast, and consistent navigation.
  • Providing text alternatives for important images and link destinations.
  • Supporting keyboard navigation for core page content and links.
  • Reviewing content structure as the site grows and changes.

Third-Party Content

Some functionality on this site may rely on third-party tools or embedded content, including the consultation form provider. While we aim to choose tools that support accessibility, we may not control every aspect of third-party interfaces.

Need Assistance?

If you have trouble using this website or need information in another format, please contact us and tell us what page or feature you were trying to use.

Ongoing Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing process. We may update this statement as the website evolves and as we make further improvements to usability, structure, and compatibility.
